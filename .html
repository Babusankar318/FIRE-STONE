About Us

One of the renowned Fire Protection and Prevention Systems Integrators in India

Founded and established in the year 2022, our professional approach in providing solutions towards optimal application, with uncompromised quality, customer support and competitive pricing have been some of the driving factors behind our accomplishments & achievements.

Firestone Fire Protection India Ltd has been in the business of Integrated Solutions since 2022. With our state-of-the-art facilities across India, we deliver quality solutions to our invaluable customers - customers who appreciate technology and can bank on a system specifically customized to meet their varied security needs.

We have our own Design Team where we prepare all working drawings according to NBC, NFPA codes & standards, TAC Norms & we are 100% responsible for all the Fire Drawings.

Our Vision

To Become "India's No. - 1, fire protection Company"

Our Mission

"Our Design, Supply and Erection of fire safety Systems, will be Value For Money Solutions, Sustainable and as per local & international Government Norms."

Chairman and Managing Director

I am pleased to present to you the Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) Manual of Firestone Fire Protection India Ltd.

I take great pride in the achievements of our employees, whose commitment and dedication continue to drive our company forward. At the same time, I believe that a large family like ours must be united by a common vision and inspired by shared values.
